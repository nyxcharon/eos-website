Embers of Solace

Posted by Bobby Martin July 1st

Embers of Solace is a 2D mmorpg that also features an offline campaign. It tells the story of the rise and fall of a great empire, and what happens to those who survived. The online portion of this game features no classes. Your fate and skills are determined by your actions. If you like to use an axe in combat, use it, but know if you later want to become a mage, you can. Do not expect to grind dungeon after dungeon in this game either. Those who strive for greatness will be rewarded, and those who chose to take a different path will be compensated as such. Often in MMOs we see grinding for equipment, when the real focus should be on the game play and story. That is what the game should be about; the story and how you experience it, not having the best equipment. This is what we are striving to achieve.


Embers of Solace is still under heavy development and design. Expect gameplay and screenshots as we continue to work hard on the game.
